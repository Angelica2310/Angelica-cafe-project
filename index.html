About us

Nestled in the heart of Hull, we’re more than just a cafe — we’re a place to connect, relax, and enjoy the little things. Our passion for high-quality, ethically-sourced coffee shines through every cup, carefully brewed by our skilled baristas. Whether you’re here for your morning pick-me-up, a cozy catch-up with friends, or a peaceful afternoon with your favorite book, we’ve got you covered.
