About us

Nestled in the heart of Hull, we’re more than just a cafe — we’re a place to connect, relax, and enjoy the little things. Our passion for high-quality, ethically-sourced coffee shines through every cup, carefully brewed by our skilled baristas. Whether you’re here for your morning pick-me-up, a cozy catch-up with friends, or a peaceful afternoon with your favorite book, we’ve got you covered. Pair your drink with our locally sourced pastries, sandwiches, or homemade cakes. We also cater to vegans and offer gluten-free options, making sure everyone feels welcome and catered for.

With free Wi-Fi, comfortable seating, and a selection of board games, Angelica's Cafe is an ideal space for work, study, or relaxation. Plus, keep an eye out for our coffee workshops and live acoustic nights, bringing together Hull’s thriving creative community. Stop by Angelica's Cafe for a coffee experience like no other!
